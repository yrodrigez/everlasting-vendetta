## Overview

The **Everlasting Vendetta** visual identity balances dark fantasy with a mystical, immersive feeling. The design embodies the guild's unique lore, characterized by its octopus mascot, Vendetto, and intricate Celtic knot motifs.

## Colors

The color palette is rooted in the guild's signature colors, evoking mysterious oceanic depths and ancient parchment.

- **Primary (#1d383b):** Deep moss green. The foundational color representing the mystical, dark fantasy atmosphere.
- **Secondary (#dbd2c3):** Pale golden beige. Used for high-contrast typography, Celtic motifs, and prominent UI elements.
- **Tertiary (#142628):** A darker shade of the moss green for layering, depth, and secondary containers.
- **Dark (#0a1314):** The absolute darkest tone for deep backgrounds.
- **Neutral (#F7F5F2):** A softer parchment for reading-heavy text blocks.
- **Accent (#a89f91):** A muted beige for disabled states or secondary text.

## Typography

The typography balances thematic flavor with modern readability.

- **Headings (Cinzel):** Cinematic and ancient to fit the mystical dark fantasy theme.
- **Body (Inter):** Clean, readable sans-serif for optimal immersion.
- **Labels (Space Grotesk):** Technical and slightly futuristic, used for UI metadata.

## Shapes

Shapes heavily feature **Celtic knot motifs**. These should serve as borders, dividers, and decorative background elements, reinforcing the ancient and mystical feel. Curves can be elegant but grounded.

## Imagery & Branding

- **Vendetto:** The octopus mascot is the core symbol of the guild's identity and should be utilized in branding and key visual moments.

## Do's and Don'ts

- **Do** lean into dark fantasy, mystical depths, and the octopus/Celtic knot aesthetics.
- **Do** use the deep moss green and pale golden beige as the primary driving colors for the UI.
- **Don't** use official World of Warcraft logos, game assets, or Horde/Alliance crests. The visual identity must remain entirely distinct and legally safe.
